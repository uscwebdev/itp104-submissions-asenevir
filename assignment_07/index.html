Project Overview

Website: This website is for a holiday home owned by my family in the hill country of my home Sri Lanka, which we open up to paying guests throughout the year. The bookings and general management of the property is handled by myself and my sister. We had been hoping to make a functioning website for guets to use, and I hope to use the skills I have learnt in this class to create this website.

Intended Audience

potenital guets looking for a place to stay in the hill country of Sri Lanka

Design

Simple and clear design, easy to follow website, with lots of pictures to showcase the property. I hope to have a Navbar with different options and subsections in a few of the main options.

Color palette

Classic design-light grey, light pink, light yellow. More of a pastel color palette.
