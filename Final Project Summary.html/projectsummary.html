Topic

ITP 104 class final project website.

This is a website for my family's holiday home we which market as a boutique hotel located in the hill country of Sri Lanka.

Multiple page layout: including a homepage, general overview page with sections describing the property and location, the different rooms available and a contact page are included in this project.

Purpose

My aim was to create a functioning website that we could use to attract potential guests by utilizing the skills I have learnt over the past semester.

I also intended to highlight the various rooms, unique location and general tranquilty that the house has to offer guests in my desgin and page content.

It was also important to me to layout important contact information to make the booking and inqury process for guests easier through this website.

Extras Used

Extras used in this project:

  1. Responsive web design: for tablets and mobile phones on all pages of the main website.
  2. Image animation: used on the overview page to highlight the about us and location section.
  3. Form:a basic contact form which includes the name, email, phone number and purpose of inquiry sections for potential users to submit.
